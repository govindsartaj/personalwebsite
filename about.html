About me

Hi there! My name is Govind, and I am a third-year/junior CS major at Grinnell College in Iowa. I am a Web Developer/Programmer at the Data Analysis and Social Inquiry Lab, Grinnell College (DASIL). Currently, I am working on an interactive chord diagram in D3.js that visualizes career paths of 8,711 Grinnell College alumni. I fixed interaction inconsistencies, redesigned the clicking/hovering logic to make the user experience more intuitive, and removed data label redundancies. I am currently working on making the visualization mobile-friendly.

In my own time, I build fun projects, like an AWS Lambda function in Python that when called, runs a scraping function that returns the names and show times of movies that are playing at the local movie theatre! I plan to extend this project by building the Google Assistant/Alexa action: “What movies are playing at The Strand right now?” Check out a complete list of projects on my projects page.
